===OPEN SPACE===

Want to hack on a project, or meet with a future collaborator? Want to present your work in an ad-hoc session? Or just hang out and watch funny cat videos?

Mark your name here to reserve an open space. You can hold your own sessions or begin working on your own projects.

Tweet #ovc11 to let people know what you're up to.

===SUNDAY EVENING SHOWCASE==

On Sunday evening it's open mic in the auditorium.

If you'd like to share progress you've made over the weekend with OVC attendees, be sure to scroll to the bottom and sign up.
